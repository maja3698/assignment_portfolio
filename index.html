What I learned during the theme:

Overall, I learned that when choosing fonts for interfaces its best to stick to only a few fonts. I also learned that when choosing colors its best to stick to either light or dark and use bright colors on links and CTAs. Secondly, I learned about animation principles and how to apply them and lastly, I learned that Grid should be used for general layout and flex for positioning, boxes and sections.
